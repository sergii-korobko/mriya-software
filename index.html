Our Values

  • Reliability: We commit to delivering robust and dependable software solutions that our clients can trust. Our dedication to quality ensures that our products consistently perform as expected, reducing downtime and enhancing operational efficiency.
  • Sustainability: We prioritize sustainable practices in our development process, creating software that is not only efficient but also environmentally friendly. Our goal is to minimize resource consumption and promote long-term sustainability in all our solutions.
  • Innovation: We embrace creativity and technological advancements to develop cutting-edge solutions that meet the evolving needs of the retail industry. By staying ahead of industry trends, we provide our clients with innovative tools that drive their success.
  • Customer-Centricity: Our clients are at the heart of everything we do. We strive to understand their unique challenges and tailor our solutions to meet their specific needs, ensuring a personalized and effective approach to every project.
  • Integrity: We conduct our business with honesty and transparency, building trust with our clients through open communication and ethical practices. Our integrity is the foundation of our long-term relationships and our reputation in the industry.

Mission

At Mriya Company, our mission is to empower the retail industry with reliable and sustainable software solutions.

We are dedicated to fostering innovation and driving efficiency, ensuring our clients can thrive in a competitive marketplace.

Through a customer-centric approach, we aim to understand and address the unique challenges of each client, delivering tailored, high-quality products that enhance their operations and support long-term success.

Guided by our core values of reliability, sustainability, innovation, integrity, and collaboration, we strive to build lasting relationships and make a positive impact on the retail sector.

About Us

Founded in 2022 in Riga, Latvia, Mriya has quickly established itself as a premier provider of top-quality software development services for the retail sector. Recognizing the unique needs and challenges of retail businesses, we set out to deliver reliable and sustainable solutions that drive efficiency and innovation.

We have focused on building strong relationships with clients in Northen Europe and beyond, leveraging our expertise to support retail customers around the world. Our commitment to excellence and our core values have been the cornerstone of our growth, allowing us to consistently exceed client expectations and make a significant impact in the retail software industry.

As we continue to evolve, our dedication to quality, customer-orientation, and technological advancement remains unwavering, ensuring that we stay at the forefront of the industry and continue to deliver exceptional solutions to our clients.
